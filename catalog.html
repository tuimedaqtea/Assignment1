Peony

The peony flower is the epitome of delicacy and grace. Peonies are known for their beauty and fragrance that fills the air with springtime notes. These flowers are associated with romance and sophistication, and they are perfect for creating delicate bouquets. Peonies are available in a variety of shades, from classic pink to deep red and white, and can bring magic to any occasion.

Peony Rose

Peony roses are the perfect combination of two gorgeous colors: roses and peonies. They have voluminous, multi-petaled buds that resemble peonies. These flowers combine the delicacy of a rose and the luxury of a peony, making them the perfect choice for special events. Peony roses are available in a variety of shades, allowing them to match any color scheme.

Rose

Roses are a symbol of love and passion, as well as a classic choice for gift giving. These flowers are known for their beauty, fragrance and symbolic meaning. Roses are available in a variety of colors, each carrying a different message: red roses express passion and love, white roses express purity and innocence, and pink roses express tenderness and charm. Roses are suitable for all occasions - from romantic gifts to congratulations and festive bouquets.
