HOW TO PLACE AN ORDER?

Send us on WhatsApp:

1. Customer's full name

2. On what date and time you need to assemble a bouquet

3. If you need delivery, please specify the exact address and time of delivery, full name and contact details of the recipient.

4. Photo of the desired bouquet.


If you want to assemble a customized order specify:

-Format(bouquet/box/composition)

-Color scheme(delicate/dark/bright)

-Your budget

-Composition wishes (for example: I want peony roses, I don't want gypsophila).

-Other wishes (note/chocolate etc).
